A. Visual group with border

A subtle blue border wraps the chained pair while dragging. Both cards fade. The drag ghost shows both cards stacked. Drop indicator appears only above or below the entire group, never between them.

B. Collapse into single ghost

While dragging, the chained pair collapses into a single faded block showing both titles. Simpler visually but loses the individual card identity. The drag preview shows a compact merged card.

C. Lock chain + single handle

Only the producing task (first in chain) gets a drag handle. The consuming task shows a lock icon instead. Dragging the producer always moves the consumer with it. Simplest to implement — no group DOM needed.
